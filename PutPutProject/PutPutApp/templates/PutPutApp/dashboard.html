Medieval
Minigolf

Welcome to Cache Valley's first interactive mini golf experience! As a Player, you can sign up for multiple sponsored tourneys, track your score, view your standing in a global leaderboard, and order drinks from the Barkeep in the Tavern. The tourney is overseen by a Manager who approves Sponsors and Barkeeps, decides when to end the tourney, and curates the Tavern's menu. The Barkeep tends a queue of drink orders and updates the drink status when an order is fulfilled. By offering this interactive mini golfing experience, Medieval Minigolf hopes to make the tournament more enjoyable and to provide you as a Player with more insight and convenience during the game.
